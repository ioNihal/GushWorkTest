Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion

High-output extruders melt and homogenize PE100 resin at controlled temperatures, forcing it through a precision die to form a continuous pipe profile.

  • Temperature-controlled barrel zones
  • Consistent melt pressure monitoring

Controlled Cooling Process

Freshly extruded pipes pass through calibrated water-cooling troughs that set the pipe's final dimensions while relieving internal stresses.

  • Multi-stage water trough cooling
  • Stress-relief and dimensional stability

Vacuum Sizing & Calibration

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and uniform wall thickness across all pipe lengths.

  • Laser-guided diameter measurement
  • Real-time wall thickness feedback

In-Line Quality Control

Every pipe undergoes continuous in-line inspection including hydrostatic pressure testing, ovality checks, and surface defect scanning before leaving the line.

  • Hydrostatic pressure validation
  • Automated surface defect detection

Pipe Marking & Identification

Inkjet printers apply permanent identification markings every metre — including brand, standard, SDR rating, pressure class, and production batch for full traceability.

  • IS/ISO standard compliant marking
  • Batch traceability codes printed

Precision Pipe Cutting

Automated planetary cutters deliver clean, square cuts to exact customer-specified lengths with no burrs, ensuring leak-free joint preparation on site.

  • Automated length measurement
  • Burr-free, square-end cutting

Secure Packaging & Dispatch

Finished pipes are bundled, end-capped, and wrapped with protective UV-resistant film. Each bundle includes a quality certificate and full traceability documentation.

  • UV-protective wrapping for storage
  • Quality certificate in every bundle
